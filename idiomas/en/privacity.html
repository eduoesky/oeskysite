How is the data used?
The data provided (e.g. email) is only used to make sure you are a human and not a robot. In the case of the likes method, it exists to check how many Pous will be used for each email provided. If you exceed 3 Pous in a single email, the likes will not be given in any way. Emails can also be used to send an automated or manual response, depending on which method was sent.
Is there a danger of being hacked?
No! Even though there is no danger, I advise you to put an email that is not linked to any Pou, as long as it is a real email. Fake emails are ignored by the system. In optional fields, you can simply choose not to send the email, but this does not mean that if you enter a false email, the system will accept it.
Note: regardless of whether the site has (or does not) the optional email, fake emails will be ignored.
